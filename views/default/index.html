Welcome

The CrowdLab Ranking System supports the ranking and reviews of submissions sent to venues. A venue can represent a homework assignment, a conference.
  • The Venues tab allows you to find venues open for submission and review. You can submit to open venues, and you can accept reviewing tasks for venues for which you can review. Site admins can also create new venues.
  • The User lists tab allows site admins to create and manage lists of users who can submit to venues or review submissions.
  • The Reviews tab lists your reviewing tasks, and allows you to enter reviews and ratings.
  • The My Submissions tab allows you to access your submissions and to view the reviews and ratings they received.

To access most functions, you need to log in with a Google account.
